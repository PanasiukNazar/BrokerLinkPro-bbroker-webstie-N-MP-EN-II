Private Data We Receive And Collect

As a partner broker, we collect and process specific data to enhance your experience with our services. This includes information about your account activity, interactions with our platform, and details related to your brokerage operations. We automatically gather technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times when you use our website.

When you register as a partner broker and utilize our services, we collect essential personal and business-related information such as:

  • Your full name, username, and email address provided during registration
  • Business details, including brokerage firm name and professional experience
  • Transaction history and performance analytics relevant to brokerage activities
  • IP addresses and login timestamps for security and verification purposes
  • Billing details required for commission payouts and financial transactions
  • User-generated content, including messages, feedback, and interaction data
  • Communication records with support and partner relationship management teams

How We Use Data

We use the collected data to enhance our partner broker services and optimize your experience on our platform:

  1. To verify your identity and provide secure access to partner broker resources
  2. To track your brokerage performance and calculate commissions accurately
  3. To process transactions and ensure financial security
  4. To analyze broker activity and improve platform efficiency
  5. To offer personalized support and updates on relevant services
  6. To maintain security and prevent unauthorized access to partner resources

Customer Content We Process

As a partner broker, you may submit data related to client interactions, trading activities, and performance metrics. We ensure the confidentiality and security of all such data, using it strictly to provide better brokerage services and improve operational efficiency.

Consent To Using Services

By using our partner broker services, you consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your data in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using our platform.

Request Your Data

If you wish to inquire about the data we have collected, please contact us through the provided channels. We ensure transparency in handling your information and respect your rights regarding data privacy.
